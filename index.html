Sometimes, it is very noticeable when people need help, sometimes it is not. Safeguarding protects adults and children at risk from harm and abuse.

Key Aspects of Safeguarding

The key aspects of safeguarding focus on protecting individuals from harm and promoting well-being. This includes preventing abuse, neglect, and exploitation while empowering individuals to have a voice in decisions affecting them. Accountability ensures clear policies are followed, and collaboration between professionals, families, and communities helps create safe, supportive environments.


Safeguarding Activities

Safeguarding activities ensure safe environments and protect vulnerable individuals from harm. These include conducting background checks on staff, training professionals to identify and respond to abuse, and establishing clear reporting procedures. Educating individuals and families about their rights and regularly monitoring environments like schools and care facilities help identify risks and maintain safety.
